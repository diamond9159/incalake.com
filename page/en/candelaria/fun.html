Fun in Puno

Bars
  • Positive Rock and reggae: Lima st. 382; purely for foreign tourists this place is a good place to dance and drink while in a small room.
Karaoke
  • Cantobar: Lima st. 378; one of the best karaokes of Puno, is located on the main street of the city on the second floor
Discotheques
  • Domino: Libertad st. 443; the safest nightclub, people normally are full on Fridays and Saturdays. Do not expect anything the rest of the week.
  • Platinum: Libertad street, at about 50 meters from the domino disco; with decent drinks and electronic music and some salsa, you can find this disco frequented on weekends by middle class people of the city of Puno
